Naamcos

Welcome to NAAMCOS, a vibrant Agricultural and Allied Multi State Cooperative Society Ltd. dedicated to empowering farmers, promoting sustainable agriculture, and fostering community development. NAAMCOS will be at the forefront of agricultural cooperation, providing vital support to our member-farmers through:

  • Input supply and marketing services
  • Technical guidance and training
  • Financial assistance and insurance
  • Market linkages and advocacy

Our goal is to:

  • Enhance agricultural productivity and quality
  • Improve farmers’ livelihoods and income
  • Promote eco-friendly practices and climate resilience
  • Foster collaboration and knowledge sharing
Our vision is to create a prosperous, sustainable, and equitable agricultural community, where farmers thrive and contribute to the nation’s food security. Join us in our mission to transform agriculture and empower rural communities.
